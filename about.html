About Us

  • Our Mission

    The First Reformed Baptist Church in Freetown, Sierra Leone, is dedicated to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ and fostering spiritual growth within our community. Led by Pastor Emmanuel Komba, we strive to provide a welcoming environment for worship and fellowship.

    We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the importance of community service. Our church seeks to spread hope and love through various outreach programs and activities.
